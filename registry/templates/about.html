Soteria is a container ‘registry’ and distribution network available to any US-based researcher and their collaborators. Containers registered on Soteria have three primary advantages: Discoverablity, Visibility and Traceability.

Discoverability

  • Easy to cite Images
  • Generation of Metadata for creation of DOIs and archival
    • Funding Agencies
    • Grant Numbers

Visibility

  • Effectively communicate container contents
  • Advertise the discovered software
    • System RPMs
    • Known vulnerabilities
